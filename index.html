Frequently Asked Questions

Is this coding platform really free?

Yes. All courses are 100% free with no payment and no account required. Start learning immediately in your browser.

Which technologies are covered?

We cover Flutter, Dart, TypeScript, React, NestJS, MongoDB, Firebase, Docker, REST APIs, Clean Architecture, BLoC state management, and modern full-stack workflows.

Is this suitable for absolute beginners?

Yes. Our Flutter course starts from "What is Programming?" and our Full-Stack course starts from the basics. No prior experience needed.

How is this different from video tutorials?

You get interactive quizzes, active recall, spaced repetition, code walkthroughs, and spot-the-bug challenges. You learn by doing, not just watching.

I am not a developer. Can Teamz Lab build my app?

Absolutely. Teamz Lab builds mobile apps (iOS & Android), web apps, SaaS platforms, and AI solutions for businesses worldwide. You bring the idea, we build the product.

How much does it cost to build a mobile app?

It depends on features and complexity. Contact us on Upwork for a free consultation and estimate. We work with startups, small businesses, and enterprises.

What technologies does Teamz Lab use to build apps?

We build with Flutter for cross-platform mobile apps, React and NestJS for web apps, Firebase and AWS for backend, and integrate AI with OpenAI, Claude, and custom ML models.

Can you build an MVP for my startup idea?

Yes. We specialize in rapid MVP development. We help you validate your idea fast with a working product, then scale it as your business grows.

Do you build apps for both iOS and Android?

Yes. Using Flutter, we build one codebase that runs beautifully on both iOS and Android, saving you time and budget. We also publish to both App Store and Play Store.

Can I hire Teamz Lab for ongoing development?

Yes. We offer dedicated development teams, ongoing maintenance, feature development, and technical support. Hire us through Upwork or contact us directly.
